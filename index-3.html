Dr Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is also an ordained Priest in Ibadan North Diocese Anglican Communion. He was made a deacon in 1999 and ordained a preist in 2001. He was preferred Canon in December 2010. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye is a kind hearted philanthropist who is interested in the welfare of the poor, the widows and the orphans. He is also interested in the cause of the down trodden. He is married with children. He is also happy, fulfilled, friendly, cheerful, jovial and Christianly. Dr. Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is interested in being connected all over the world for Lecturing appointment in Religious Studies as well as Preistly duties in churches all over the world. He is also interested in Humanitarian duties like orphanages old peoples homes, ministering to prisoners, visiting the sick in the hospitals and hospices.
