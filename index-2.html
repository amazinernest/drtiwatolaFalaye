Dr. Tiwatola Falaye attended the University of Ibadan from 1980 to 1983. He graduated with a B.A. Honours in Religious Studies. He proceeded for the masters degree in 1984 and graduated in 1986 specialising in Philosophy of Religion in the same University. Dr Tiwatola Falaye started the Ph.D programme in the University of Ibadan in 1993 and completed successfuly in the year 2005 specialising in Church History. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye has worked as a school teacher in New Church Modern School Owo in 1976. He has also taught in Amunye High School Owo in 1980. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye started his career as a lecturer in the Federal college of Education Katsina in December 1984 and and stopped there in 1991. He started University lecturing appointment in october 1991 at Olabisi Onabanjo University Ago Iwoye up to date. Dr. Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is currently a Senior lecturer in Religious Studies in Olabisi Onabanjo University Ago Iwoye, Ogun State, Nigeria.
