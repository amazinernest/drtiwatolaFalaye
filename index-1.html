Dr. Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye was born to the family of Late Pa Jonathn Adebiyi Falaye and Madam Janet Oluwakemi Adetutu Falaye on the 16th of september 1959 in Owo, Ondo state Nigeria. He attended St Andrews Primary School Owo, Ondo state Nigeria from 1965 to 1970. He also attended St. Patricks College Oka, Ondo State Nigeria from 1971 - 1975. There he proceeded to Baptist Acedemy Lagos for A level or H.S.C. between 1977 and 1979. He was the Senior Prefect in 1979. For University education Dr. Tiwatola Falaye attended the University of Ibadan from 1980 to 1983. He graduated with a B.A. Honours in Religious Studies. He proceeded for the masters degree in 1984 and graduated in 1986 specialising in Philosophy of Religion in the same University. Dr Tiwatola Falaye started the Ph.D programme in the University of Ibadan in 1993 and completed successfuly in the year 2005 specialising in Church History. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye has worked as a school teacher in New Church Modern School Owo in 1976. He has also taught in Amunye High School Owo in 1980. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye started his career as a lecturer in the Federal college of Education Katsina in December 1984 and and stopped there in 1991. He started University lecturing appointment in october 1991 at Olabisi Onabanjo University Ago Iwoye up to date. Dr. Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is currently a Senior lecturer in Religious Studies in Olabisi Onabanjo University Ago Iwoye, Ogun State, Nigeria. Dr Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is also an ordained Priest in Ibadan North Diocese Anglican Communion. He was preferred Canon in December 2010. Dr. Tiwatola Falaye is a kind hearted philanthropist who is interested in the welfare of the poor, the widows and the orphans. He is also interested in the cause of the down trodden. He is married with children. He is also happy, fulfilled, friendly, cheerful, jovial and Christianly. Dr. Tiwatola Abidemi Falaye is interested in being connected all over the world for Lecturing appointment in Religious Studies as well as Preistly duties in churches all over the world. He is also interested in Humanitarian duties like orphanages old peoples homes, ministering to prisoners, visiting the sick in the hospitals and hospices.
